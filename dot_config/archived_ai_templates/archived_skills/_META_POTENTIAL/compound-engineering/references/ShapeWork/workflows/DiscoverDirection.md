# DiscoverDirection

Use this workflow when the request still needs problem framing, requirements, or repo context.

## Route Selection

| Need | Load |
|---|---|
| Generate improvement ideas, compare opportunities, or ask "what should we improve" | `../../ce-ideate/MetaSkill.md` |
| Explore a vague feature, clarify scope, or produce a requirements document | `../../ce-brainstorm/MetaSkill.md` |
| Understand the codebase before deciding what to do next | `../../onboarding/MetaSkill.md` |

## Expected Result

- A clearer problem frame
- A narrowed direction or ranked options
- A durable requirements or onboarding artifact when needed
