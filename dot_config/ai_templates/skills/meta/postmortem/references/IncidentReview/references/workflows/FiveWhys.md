# FiveWhys

Use when the user wants a short root-cause chain.

## Method

1. State the observed failure.
2. Ask why it happened.
3. Repeat until the chain reaches a systemic explanation or the next why stops adding signal.
4. Stop before the chain becomes speculative.
5. End with corrective actions tied to the deepest supported cause.

## Output

```text
Problem:

Why 1:
Why 2:
Why 3:
Why 4:
Why 5:

Corrective Actions:
```
