## Tool Selection by Scenario

**Fast company baseline**: OpenCorporates, Crunchbase, LinkedIn (leadership), BuiltWith, Google News.

**Vendor due diligence**: OpenCorporates + SEC EDGAR (registration), PACER + CourtListener (legal), OFAC + OpenSanctions (sanctions), Crunchbase + D&B (financial), LinkedIn + ZoomInfo (leadership).

**Nonprofit research**: IRS Select Check, ProPublica Nonprofit Explorer (990s), GuideStar/Candid, Charity Navigator, USAspending (government grants).

**Investment due diligence**: All vendor sources plus PitchBook, SimilarWeb, BuiltWith, Glassdoor (employee sentiment), patent databases, and breach exposure checks.
