# Secret History #25 — Capital of Evil

**Source:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B5FtHagng8c
**Series:** Predictive History — Secret History

## Overview

Redefines capital as a mechanism to extract attention and focus, not mere wealth. Three extraction levers: the illusion of freedom (freemen outperform slaves, equity keeps them focused), the abstraction of capital (grain → gold → money as never-enough god), and engineered anxiety (debt, visible poverty, and cyclical wealth destruction through war and crashes). When populations push back, landlords become transnational capital and move — powered by secret societies that use Milgram-style authority and Asch-style conformity to license acts individuals couldn't otherwise commit.

Secret societies are built by trauma (the kidnapped wife, Spartan agoge, the hashashin's drug-sex-trauma loop) that makes members psychological slaves of their handlers. Transnational capital migrates in four stages: Venice (merchant oligarchy + spice trade), the Dutch Republic (Calvin's double predestination + Dutch East India Company), England (Bank of England 1694, Napoleonic-war financing, East India Company's $45T extraction of India by co-opting local elites), and America (Vanderbilt, Carnegie, Rockefeller, Morgan as British-capital fronts, Federal Reserve 1913, Nixon's 1971 gold-decoupling, the petrodollar). Freemason symbolism on US currency and "Novus Ordo Seclorum" close the argument.

- Capital redefined as a mechanism to extract human attention and focus
