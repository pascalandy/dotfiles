# Secret History #5 — The Birth of Evil

**Source:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EgGfQdv_rPc
**Series:** Predictive History — Secret History

## Overview

The second lecture on secret societies: where they come from and what they believe. The lecturer walks through three stages of Western religion — mother goddess civilization (agriculture, womb-centered, women in charge), polytheism (war-driven pantheons), and monotheism (empire-driven, Roman) — and argues that the great suppressed secret is that mind leads to matter, not the reverse. Women's fertility cults went underground into mystery schools, then into secret societies carrying this knowledge forward.

The second half rereads the Bible esoterically through the Book of Enoch and the Gospel of Thomas. Sophia creates the Demiurge, who becomes the false god of Genesis; the Nephilim still rule the world. Genesis itself, read literally, supports Satan's claim that God was lying about the fruit. Milton's Paradise Lost is presented as a secret-society text embedding this cosmology, with Satan's speeches as initiation scripts. The truth sits inside each person as a divine spark connected to the Monad.

## Key themes

- Three-stage arc: mother goddess → polytheism → monotheism, each driven by material conditions
- The great suppressed secret: mind leads to matter, not matter to mind
- Metaphorical vs. literal worldview and the loss of ancient creativity
- Pantheon formation: defeated societies' gods absorbed as children of the victor's god
- Monotheism's three counterintuitive concepts: truth, evil, the individual
- Fertility cults → mystery schools → secret societies (Templars, Rosicrucians, Illuminati, Masons)
- Sophia, the Demiurge, and the Bible's God as false god / prison warden
- The Nephilim as offspring of angels and human women still running the world
- Genesis reread: two trees, Satan telling the truth, divine spark inside Eve
- Paradise Lost: Milton's Satan speeches as soul-ascent, birth metaphor, and initiation script
