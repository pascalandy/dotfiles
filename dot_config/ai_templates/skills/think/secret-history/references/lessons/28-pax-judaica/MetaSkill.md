# Secret History END — Pax Judaica

**Source:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WFWizN3QoPg
**Series:** Predictive History — Secret History

## Overview

The course's grand synthesis. A theory approaches truth if it connects the past, explains the present, and predicts the future. From there the lecturer tells a single story: Jacob Frank, Mayer Rothschild, and Adam Weishaupt seed the Illuminati, which infiltrates Freemasonry and binds Jesuit, Jewish, Catholic, and Frankist networks under a secret leadership of "thirteen families" coordinated by four overlapping eschatologies — Jewish, Sabbatean Frankist, Christian Zionist, and Freemason — all converging on Jerusalem.

Freemason deism supplies three steps to godhood (warp matter = the bomb; kill a king = JFK; create life = AI), all on the 33rd parallel from Trinity and Hiroshima/Nagasaki to Dallas to Jerusalem. The plan — drawn from Isaac Newton's Observations Upon the Prophecy of Daniel and Ezekiel 37–38 — runs rebuilding Israel, forcing diaspora return via antisemitism, rebuilding Solomon's Temple (red heifers already sacrificed), the War of Gog and Magog, and Pax Judaica: transnational capital, multinationals, and intelligence agencies headquartered in Jerusalem. The lecturer applies the frame to current events (War on Terror, immigration, AI data centers as surveillance, October 7), reads Silicon Valley as a military-tech front, connects transhumanism to Frankist flesh-over-spirit, lays out the Koestler Khazar theory, and closes with Bakunin-style resistance and favorite English poets beyond Milton (Keats, Shakespeare).
