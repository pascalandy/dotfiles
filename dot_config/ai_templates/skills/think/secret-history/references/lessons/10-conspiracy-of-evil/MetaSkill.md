## Overview

Numerology (33, triangular numbers), the 33rd-parallel line from Mesopotamia to Dallas to Trinity to Hiroshima/Nagasaki, Harry Truman as 33rd president and 33rd-degree Freemason, and NASA's Masonic roster anchor the pattern. Three steps to godhood — change matter (the bomb), kill other gods (JFK), create life (AI) — culminate in the Third Temple and a world government.
