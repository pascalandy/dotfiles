# Secret History #26 — Faith of Evil

**Source:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kULUM_eB8KI
**Series:** Predictive History — Secret History

## Overview

Traces Jewish history from Persian-engineered identity through the Second Temple, Maccabean revolt, Rome's 70 CE destruction, rabbinic Judaism, medieval nobility's scapegoat arrangement, Al-Andalus, the 1492 expulsion, and massacres across the Polish-Lithuanian Commonwealth. Centuries of persecution generate three unanswerable theological questions and a crisis of faith that sets up a messianic era.

Sabbatai Zevi declares himself Messiah, demolishes rabbinic authority, introduces "justification by sin," and — when forced to convert to Islam — reframes his conversion as redemption for all converts (the Dönme, who later shape Turkey). Fifty years later, Jacob Frank claims to be Zevi reincarnated and systematizes Sabbatian Frankism: a flat brotherhood converting to Catholicism, accelerating the messianic age through sin, and training imagination to conquer the material world. Frank's twelve public teaching-stories (the Sabbath knife, the plague town, four brothers vs. the bear, the pearl and the apprentice, rewriting Eden and Augustine) encode manipulation, fearlessness, love, and the illusory nature of convention. Eva Frank as Messiah, the Big Brother eschatology, Brandeis, Goethe's Faust, and Yeats's Crazy Jane show the reach into modernity.

## Key themes

- Persian construction and Roman destruction of Jewish identity; temple-to-rabbi shift
- Maccabean revolt, Hanukkah, Rome's 70 CE burning of the Second Temple
- Medieval arrangement: Jews as bankers, merchants, and scapegoats for European nobility
- Al-Andalus prosperity, 1492 Spanish expulsion, and the Polish-Lithuanian refuge
- Three unanswerable theological questions generating a crisis of faith
- Sabbatai Zevi: egalitarianism, anti-rabbinic, justification by sin, Islamic conversion, Dönme
- Jacob Frank as Zevi reincarnated; Catholic conversion; flat-hierarchy brotherhood
- Twelve teaching-stories (Sabbath knife, plague town, four brothers, pearl, Eden rewrite)
- Accelerationism: sin defies Satan's world, hastens the messianic age; physical immortality promised
- Frankist reach: Illuminati rumor, Goethe's Faust, Brandeis, Yeats's Crazy Jane, modern Western culture
