# Secret History #27 — Empire of Evil

**Source:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZPrecJHUOUs
**Series:** Predictive History — Secret History

## Overview

Argues the British Empire and the Sabbatean Frankist diaspora operated as a joint system: Britain's maritime-supremacy-plus-heartland-fear grand strategy and the Frankists' intelligence networks, transnational capital, and revolutionary cells reinforced each other, with Jews serving as middle managers who absorbed public blame while profits flowed upward. Case studies: Ottoman decline (forced loans, foreign-controlled taxation, Dönme-financed Young Turks), Rothschild mythology (the Waterloo stock-panic trick), and the instrumentalization of British philosophy (Locke's tabula rasa, Hume's attack on induction, Bentham's utility, Mill's liberalism, Darwin's instant canonization) as an imperial ideology that removes God and truth.

Disraeli's 1844 novel Coningsby, via the character Sidonia, openly articulates an elite-Jewish counter-network. The Bolshevik Revolution is read through Richard Spence's Wall Street and the Russian Revolution — Trotsky's New York banker funding, Red-Terror-looted valuables shipped to Wall Street, and Stalin's eventual purge of Bolshevik Jews when capital moves on. Marx is placed in a Frankist orbit (Engels, vanguardism, object-as-mirror materialism) while Bakunin's mutual-recognition alternative is presented as the suppressed road. Finally, Jeffrey Masson's work on Freud: the seduction theory (real childhood abuse) is reversed into the Oedipus complex under Frankist pressure, turning psychology into institutional gaslighting.
