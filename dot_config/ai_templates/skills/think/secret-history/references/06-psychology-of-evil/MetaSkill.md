# Secret History #6 — The Psychology of Evil

**Source:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6PdfD44DkqA
**Series:** Predictive History — Secret History

## Overview

A speculative lecture on how ancient civilizations, especially Egypt, engineered human minds through trauma and dissociation — and how the same "script" resurfaces in modern intelligence programs. After reviewing three perceptual shifts (mind vs. matter, metaphorical vs. literal, intuitive vs. counterintuitive), the lecturer compares the four earliest civilizations' solutions to social control and zooms in on Egypt's problem: the pharaoh is a human forced to act as god.

Egyptian mythology is reframed as a programming script. Ra, Osiris, and Horus are three trained identities; Isis is the controller who plants post-hypnotic suggestions via scent triggers while the drugged pharaoh is ritually stabbed, entombed, cut, and drowned. The same techniques — reframed as "enhanced interrogation" and learned helplessness — appear in Abu Ghraib and Guantanamo (Abu Zubaydah's testimony), and in MK Ultra. The lecturer suggests ISIS is the program's real output and that positive psychology, social media, and psychiatric medication now scale the same logic.
