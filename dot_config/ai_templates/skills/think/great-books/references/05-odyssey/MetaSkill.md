# Great Books #5 — The Odyssey

**Source:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gXlcR7uHHdA
**Series:** Predictive History — Great Books

## Overview

Introduction to the *Odyssey* as a story of trauma and the soul's journey home. The lecture reframes the poem as a family story and a psychological portrait of a broken man whose worldview has collapsed — setting up the homecoming and reconciliation that follows in lecture #6.

## Key themes

- The Odyssey as a family story, not just an adventure
- Homer's central concern: the human condition
- The soul and the worldview
- Why Odysseus goes to war — his pre-Troy worldview
- Iliad interlude: Agamemnon's reverse psychology
- The broken Odysseus: how the *Odyssey* begins
- The banquet scene as confronting trauma
- The collapse of the hero's worldview
