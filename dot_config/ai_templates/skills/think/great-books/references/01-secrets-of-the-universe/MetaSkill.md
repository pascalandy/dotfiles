# Great Books #1 — Secrets of the Universe

**Source:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TsD-8FGA84A
**Series:** Predictive History — Great Books

## Overview

The introductory lecture of the Great Books course. The instructor lays out a metaphysical framework: the universe is fundamentally *conscious*, not material; great books are vehicles for accessing deeper truths about being human; and humans can move toward immortality, reincarnation, and godhood through **love** (the unifying force) and **imagination** (the animating force).

## Key themes

- The "great lie" of materialist education — science cannot answer *what is consciousness?*
- Two aspects of being human: body + soul (with a divine spark)
- Kant: noumena vs. phenomena; Julian Jaynes's brain hemispheres as physical analogue
- The monad, vibration, and infinite-dimensional consciousness
- Plato's Allegory of the Cave → escape via meditation and sacred geometry
- "Hacks" / shortcuts to expanded consciousness
- Christ consciousness and active participation in reality
- Three goals: immortality, reincarnation, godhood
- Great books as *spiritual technology*
