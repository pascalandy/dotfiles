This category covers how workflows and methodologies are organized in this repo — today, primarily the `pa-sdlc` family under `dot_config/ai_templates/skills/pa-sdlc/`. Workflow pages here are **personal-preferences**: they describe how this operator sequences discovery, scoping, direction-setting, architecture, implementation, and documentation. A stranger using the same skills would still benefit from reading them, but they are authored from a first-person "how I use this" perspective rather than as a neutral reference.

For the skills themselves, see the `SKILL.md` files under `dot_config/ai_templates/skills/pa-sdlc/`. This wiki is the narrative layer — when and why to reach for each stage, and how they connect.
